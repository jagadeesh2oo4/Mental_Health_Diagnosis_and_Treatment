Overview

Welcome to the Mental Health Detection system. Use the tabs above to explore features. Below are the core objectives and outcomes of this project.

Objective 1 — Voice & Text Emotion Analysis

Outcome: Build AI models to analyze voice modulations and textual expressions for signs of anxiety, depression, and stress.
Research scope: Explore transformer-based models for cross-modal sentiment and emotion analysis (voice + text).
Standards: Align with WHO mental health guidelines and GDPR for privacy & data protection.

Objective 2 — Facial Expression Recognition

Outcome: Develop deep learning models to detect micro-expressions and facial indicators of mental distress from camera feeds or videos.
Research scope: Study hybrid CNN + attention architectures for robust facial recognition and micro-expression detection.
Standards: Follow applicable facial-recognition standards such as ISO/IEC 30137.

Objective 3 — AI-Powered Therapeutic Chatbot

Outcome: Design an LLM-based chatbot for supportive, non-intrusive therapeutic interactions.
Research scope: Investigate reinforcement learning and response-ranking techniques to improve empathy and safety of responses.
Standards: Ensure ethical AI alignment (e.g., IEEE P7001) and safety guardrails for mental-health use.

Objective 4 — Behavioral Data Analytics

Outcome: Analyze longitudinal user behavior to provide actionable insights to therapists and caregivers.
Research scope: Explore predictive analytics and time-series modeling to track mental health trends.
Standards: Adhere to ISO standards for statistical data & metadata exchange (e.g., ISO 13120).

Objective 5 — Cloud Deployment & Scalability

Outcome: Deploy the system on cloud (AWS/Azure) with secure encryption and access controls for scalability.
Research scope: Study cost-efficient, secure deployment strategies for AI-driven mental-health applications.
Standards: Comply with cloud security standards such as ISO/IEC 27001.
